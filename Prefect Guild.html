About Us

The Sri Khemananda Daham Pasala Prefect Guild is dedicated to fostering an environment of discipline, leadership, and mindfulness among students. Comprising diligent and exemplary individuals, the Guild plays a pivotal role in upholding the values of the Daham Pasala, guiding younger students, and organizing various spiritual and community-focused activities. We believe in nurturing well-rounded individuals who embody service and responsibility.
